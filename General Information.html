General Information

About Pompeii

  • Located in coastal southwest Italy
  • Pompeii as we can see it now symbolized the Roman Empire at its height
  • The people of Pompeii didn't know that Mount Vesuvius was a volcano
  • Researchers believe that the town was founded in the seventh or sixth century BC by the Osci or Oscans
  • Pompeii became a Roman colony after they joined an unsuccessful rebellion against the Roman Republic and were conquered
  • Evidence for the destruction originally came from a surviving letter by Pliny the Younger
  • "'Darkness fell, not the dark of a moonless or cloudy night, but as if the lamp had been put out in a dark room,' wrote
    Pliny the Younger, who witnessed the cataclysm from across the Bay of Naples" (Owens).
  • The site was lost for about 1,600 years until its initial rediscovery in 1709
  • Today it has UNESCO World Heritage Site status
  • Pompeii played a major role in the neo-classical revival of the 18th century
