Life in Pompeii

  • Mice were a delicacy in Pompeii
  • Pompeii attracted wealthy vacationers
  • Pompeii was a fluorishing resort for Rome's most distinguished citizens
  • Most walls had murals or frescoes
  • Frescoes were paintings on wet plaster, which made them more durable
  • Mosaics were tiny pieces of glass, stone, and other materials that were arranged so that they made an image
