Influence on Modern Times

Pompeii's impact on today

  • Pompeii is considered the most important archaeological site in the world for gaining information about ancient civilizations.
  • Pompeii is a 'window into the past' that shows what the Roman empire was like during the Pax Romana
  • Resources from Pompeii are very important for teaching people today about the ancient times
  • Today it is a World Heritage Site to help people grasp how important the Roman Empire was for our world today
